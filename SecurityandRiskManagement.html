Security and Risk Management

Tutor: Dr. Nawaz Khan

Learning Objectives


* Describe when to use qualitative and quantitative risk assessments..
* Create threat models for various scenarios.
* Create quantitative risk models.
* Design and explain DR strategies and solutions.

Unit 1 - An Introduction to Security and
Risk Management

This unit has enabled me to:
* Discuss the various definitions of risk.
* Explain how to assess, qualify and mitigate risks.
* Describe various approaches to quantify and qualify risks.
* List common security and risk standards and select the appropriate one(s) for a given situation.

  • Artefact
  • Collaborative Discussion 1

Unit 2 - Users Assessment and the
Rist Management Process

This unit has enabled me to:
* Discuss the differences between assessment approaches (Qualitative vs. Quantitative).
* Explain the importance of user participation in the risk management process.
* Discuss the implications of any recommended mitigations.

  • Seminar
  • Discussion 1 - Peer Review

Unit 3 - Introduction to Threat Modelling
and Management

This unit has enabled me to:
* Describe a number of threat modelling techniques.
* Advise which technique should be used in specific situations.
* Discuss when techniques should be combined in a hybrid model.

  • Artefact

Unit 4 - Application of Threat Modelling and
Management Techniques

This unit has enabled me to:
* Describe where to find the key tools and resources used for threat modelling.
* Explain how to select the most appropriate tool for various situations.
* Critically evaluate the use of such tools.

  • Seminar

Unit 5 - An Introduction to Security and Risk
Standards in Industry and the Enterprise

This unit has enabled me to:
* List common security standards and select the appropriate one(s) for a given situation.
* Describe how to allow for standards in typical threat models.
* Review a number of common industry and enterprise standards.

Unit 6 - The Practical Implications of Security
and Risk Standards

This unit has enabled me to:
* Explain which GDPR regulations are applicable in specific situations.
* Describe which other standards might apply in selected situations.
* Advise on mitigations to help a business meet any of the standards applicable to its specific industry (such as data and privacy (GDPR) or financial (PCI-DSS)).

  • Group Assignment

Unit 7 - An Introduction to the Concepts of
Quantitative Risk Modelling

This unit has enabled me to:
* Utilise quantitative risk modelling techniques to produce risk models.
* Select the most appropriate technique based on the real-world problem.
* Critically evaluate the pros and cons of the approach selected.

  • Artefact
  • Collaborative Discussion 2

Unit 8 - Implementing Quantitative Risk Models

This unit has enabled me to:
* Select the most appropriate modelling technique to use in a given situation.
* Create a QR model of a real-world situation.
* Provide a critical appraisal of the use of QR techniques.

Unit 9 - Risk, Business Continuity and Disaster
Recovery

This unit has enabled me to:
* Explain what is meant by BC, DR, RTO and RPO.
* Suggest the most suitable solutions to meet specific recovery objectives.
* Discuss some of the factors driving the future of the Information Risk Management field.

  • Artefact

Unit 10 - Practical Applications and Issues in
DR Implementations

This unit has enabled me to:
* Design a solution that will meet a set of RPO and RTO requirements.
* Describe the advantages and disadvantages of DRaaS.
* Discuss the challenges with vendor lock-in, resilience and (network) security.

  • Seminar

Unit 11 - Future Trends in Security and Risk
Management

This unit has enabled me to:
* Describe the main emerging trends in the field.
* Explain their potential impact on research.
* Recommend which you think will be most influential.

  • Artefact
  • Project: Executive Summary

Unit 12 - The Great Debate: What will be the most
influential trend in SRM in the next 5 years?

This unit has enabled me to:
* Describe the main emerging trends in the field.
* Argue for which you believe to be the most influential.
* Cast your vote for the trend you support.
